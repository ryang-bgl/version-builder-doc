Privacy Policy for Issue Status Time Analyzer

Effective Date: 2025-07-12

Issue Status Time Analyzer ("us", "we", or "our") is a plugin for Atlassian Jira product.

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

By using the Issue Status Time Analyzer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

The Issue Status Time Analyzer plugin is designed with privacy in mind and collects minimal configuration data necessary for its operation:

  • Working Hours Configuration: Your customized business hours and working days settings to calculate accurate time measurements.
  • Effort Estimation File Name: Where the user store the estimation effort in jira.
  • Statues to Analyze: Which status the user wants to analyze.

No personally identifiable information (PII), issue content, or sensitive project data is collected or stored by the plugin.

2. How We Use the Information

The collected configuration data is used solely to:

  • Provide accurate time calculations based on your business hours and timezone.
  • Display analytics according to your configured status and preferences.
  • Remember your filter settings and dashboard configurations for a better user experience.
  • Ensure consistent reporting across your workflow analysis sessions.

This configuration data is stored within your Atlassian Jira Cloud environment and is not transmitted to any external servers or third-party services.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All configuration data is stored within your Atlassian Jira Cloud using standard Atlassian storage mechanisms.

No External APIs: Issue Status Time Analyzer does not make any calls to external APIs or third-party services. All data processing occurs within your Jira environment.

No Data Transmission: Your configuration data never leaves your Jira instance and is not shared with us or any third parties.

Atlassian Security: Your data security is governed by Atlassian's security policies and your organization's Jira configuration.

4. What We Don't Collect

Issue Status Time Analyzer explicitly does NOT collect:

  • Issue content, descriptions, or comments
  • User personal information or credentials
  • Project data or sensitive business information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • User behavior tracking or monitoring data
  • Any data from external systems or integrations

5. Data Access and Control

Your Control: You have complete control over your configuration data through the plugin's settings interface.

Data Deletion: You can reset or change your configuration settings at any time through the plugin interface.

Uninstallation: Uninstalling the plugin will remove all associated configuration data from your Jira instance according to Atlassian's data policy.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time, and updated the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@avolink.io

8. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Atlassian Marketplace privacy requirements
  • Industry best practices for data protection

Since Issue Status Time Analyzer operates entirely within your Jira environment and doesn't collect personal data, compliance requirements are primarily handled by your organization's Jira instance configuration.

Privacy Summary

Issue Status Time Analyzer is designed with privacy as a core principle. We only store minimal configuration data (working hours, status groupings, etc.) locally in your Jira Cloud. No personal data, issue content, or external API calls are involved. Your data stays completely within your control and your Jira environment.
